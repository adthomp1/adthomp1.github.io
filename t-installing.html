A setup guide to downloading and installing LibreOffice 4.3

Context

This guide is meant for users of Windows 7, other versions of Windows may not follow the exact process described

Download and Installation Instructions

  1. Go to http://www.libreoffice.org/
  2. Click "Download Now" on the home page to be taken to the download page. (Figure 1)
  3. Click "Download Version 4.3.0" to begin the download. (Figure 2)
  4. The installer should begin downloading. If it does not, click on "LibreOffice_4.3.0_Win_x86.msi" to begin the download. (Figure 3)
  5. Once the installer has completed downloading, open the installer.
  6. A pop-up may appear with a security warning. If it does, click "Run" to proceed. (Figure 4)
  7. The Installation Wizard will now step you through the installation process. Click "Next" to proceed. (Figure 5)
  8. The wizard will give you the options to install with a Typical or Custom Setup. For most users, the Typical Setup will be sufficient. Click "Next" to proceed. (Figure 6)
  9. Click "Install" to begin the installation. (Figure 7)
  10. Once the installation has completed, click "Finish" to close the Installation Wizard.

LibreOffice 4.3 has now been installed successfully!
