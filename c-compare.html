To many people, the terms open source and free are associated with a lack of features and support. Fortunately, this is not the case with LibreOffice. Almost anything you can do with Microsoft Office, you can do with LibreOffice, and more!

Let's look at what's contained in LibreOffice:

  • Writer - a full featured word processor, comparable to MS Word
  • Calc - a spreadsheet program, comparable to MS Excel
  • Impress - the presentation creator, comparable to MS Power Point
  • Base - a powerful database front end, comparable to MS Access

LibreOffice also contains two more pieces of software that are not analogous to anything contained in MS Office:

  • Draw - a drawing a flowchart application
  • Math - an equation editor

For a full feature comparison between LibreOffice and Microsoft Office, click here.

In addition, LibreOffice is totally open source and free! There are even versions that can be run from a USB drive and don't have to be installed on the computer at all.
